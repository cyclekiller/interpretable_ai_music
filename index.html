Music generation quality comparison

注：样本生成方式
先用 notagen 模型随机生成若干首歌曲，从中截取包含 3–4 个声部的片段；保留 notagen 随机生成的第一声部（旋律），其余声部及和声进行由我们的模型重新创作。 因此每一组对比（如 1_notagen.mp3 vs 1_ours.mp3）都是在同一段旋律上展开的。
所有音频均通过 MuseScore 由 MIDI 渲染：统一钢琴音色、统一音符力度，无额外混音处理。
Note: Sample generation method
First the NotaGen model produced several songs; we picked clips that contain 3–4 voices. Keeping the first voice (melody) that NotaGen randomly generated, we composed the remaining voices and the harmony progression with our model. Thus every comparison (e.g. 1_NotaGen.mp3 vs 1_ours.mp3) starts from the same melody.
All audio was rendered from MIDI with MuseScore: identical piano sound, identical note velocities, no extra mixing.
